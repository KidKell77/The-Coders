🌱 Planting Guide

🌽 Maize: Rows 20–30 cm apart, well‑drained, water every 2–3 days, harvest 90–120 days.
🥬 Spinach: Cool weather, harvest 30–40 days, water regularly.
🥕 Carrot: Sandy soil, keep moist, harvest 70–80 days.
🍅 Tomato: Full sun, stake plants, water deeply every 2 days.
🥬 Cabbage: Full sun, fertile soil, harvest 60–90 days.
🫑 Green Pepper: Warm soil, start indoors, transplant after 6 weeks.
🍠 Sweet Potato: Loose warm soil, harvest 90–120 days.
🧅 Onion: Seed or sets, full sun, harvest 90–110 days.
